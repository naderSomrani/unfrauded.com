About Us

Unfrauded is a Tunisian Startup founded in August 2019 by 4 young computer engineers. The story started after they won the Tunisia Digital Summit Hackathon, this resulted in a collaboration with the largest insurance company in the country to build Unfrauded. In may of 2020 V1.0 of Unfrauded was launched at STAR and the company was recognized as a promasing startup getting awarded the Startup Act Label.

Unfrauded E-Claim

An easy and guided way to file for a claim, keep track of its progress and exchange with everyone involved in the claim handeling all with your phone.
This mobile application not only provides a faster service and a better customer experience for policyholders but also contributes to a better fraud detection for the insurance companies, through a back office module analyzing the input by the policyholders.

Accelerating Claim Processing

Verify User Inputs

The app retrieves policy data from the insurer's servers insuring accurate claim filling.


Accident Photos

The user can add photos of the accident and indicate the type of damage present in each photo as well as the accident location using google maps.

Instant Messaging

The users can communicate with their insurer or the damage assessment expert in charge of handeling their claim.

Generate PDF claim

The user can generate a PDF that can be printed if needed
